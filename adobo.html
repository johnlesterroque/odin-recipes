Adobong Baboy

Ingredients

  • 6 pcs prok belly cut into cubes
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup of cane vinger
  • 1 teaspoon dark soy sauce
  • a pinch of salt
  • a pinch of sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 onion sliced
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • laurel leaves

Steps

  1. Fry in a hot pan the pork belly until skin is golden brown.
  2. Fry the garlic and onion.
  3. Put everything together except the vinegar, lower the heat, cook for 30 to 40 minutes until chicken is cooked.
  4. Put the vinger last and serve with rice.
