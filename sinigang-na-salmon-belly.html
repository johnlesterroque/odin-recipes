Sinigang na Salmon Belly

Ingredients

  • 6 pcs salmon belly
  • 2 teaspoon Mama Sita's sinigang powder mix
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 whole onion head
  • 5 pcs of tomatoes
  • water
  • a lot of Spinach

Steps

  1. Put everything together in a pan to boil.
  2. Boil in low-heat for 30 minutes or until cooked.
  3. Serve with rice.
