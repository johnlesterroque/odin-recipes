Baked Chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 pcs chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon red paprika powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme powder
  • 1 teaspoon oregano powder
  • 1 teaspoon rosemarry powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspon olive oil/li>

Steps

  1. Marinade the chicken with all the herbs, garlic and olive oil, pre-heat the oven in 180 degree celcius
  2. Put the marinated chicken in the oven and cook for 30 minutes or until cooked.
  3. Open the oven and turn the chicken upside down and wait for another 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked.
  4. Serve with rice.
